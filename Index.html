Subnautica: The Lost Expedition

Five years ago your father disappeared.

His name was Simon.

A legendary deep-sea explorer who travelled across dangerous worlds searching for alien oceans. One day his last message came from a remote planet almost entirely covered in water.

Then the signal stopped.

No rescue team ever returned.

Everyone else gave up.

But you didn’t.

Your name is Max. And today your small exploration ship arrives at the same planet your father vanished on.

Planet designation: 4546-B

Suddenly alarms scream through the cockpit. Your ship begins falling toward the endless ocean below.

Your ship crashes.

You wake up inside a damaged escape pod floating on an alien sea.

Your radio flickers with a weak signal.

A message repeats over and over:

"...Max... if you're hearing this... follow the signal..."

It’s your father’s voice.

What do you do?
